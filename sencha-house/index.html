I · The Standard

Before anything is designed, the tea has already been decided. Selection, blending, roasting: a discipline refined over decades by people who answer to a standard most producers never reach.

II · Cultural Translation

Listening comes first. Expression comes only after. We do not import Japan and set it down elsewhere; we find the form that lets its values resonate here, now, for the people in front of us.

III · Tea Design

Formats that don't have a name yet, built around discovery, wellness, connection, vitality. Whatever the shape, the standard underneath it does not move. Only the form does.

IV · Other Hands

Innovation has never been a solitary act. The bowl a tea is served in comes from a ceramicist held to the same standard we hold the leaf to. We are continuing what was already there.
