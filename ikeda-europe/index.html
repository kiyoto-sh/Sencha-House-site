Manifest 03 — Origin
Kagoshima, Japan
Rank #1 · Japan, 2024

Volcanic soil, subtropical climate, and elevation give Kagoshima tea its distinct character. In 2024 the prefecture overtook Shizuoka to become Japan's largest tea-producing region by volume. Ikeda Seicha has cultivated here since 1945.
